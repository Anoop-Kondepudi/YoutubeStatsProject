This project allows you to upload your YouTube statistics and provides insightful data about your time spent on the platform.

Let's begin

First, go to Google Takeout.

Next, click the "Deselect all" button to deselect everything from your Google account export options.

Select only YouTube and YouTube Music data and then click "Next Step" to continue.
Note: The "YouTube and YouTube Music" option is near the bottom of the step 2 list.

Finally, click the "Create export" button to start the process. You will receive an email once your export is ready to download.

The export process may take anywhere from a few minutes to several hours, depending on the amount of data in your YouTube account. Once you receive your export, click the "Get your YouTube Stats Now" button below to continue and upload your zip file.
